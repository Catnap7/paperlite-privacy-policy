PDF Scanner - PaperLite

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

PDF Scanner - PaperLite is a local document scanning app.

Summary

PaperLite does not require an account and does not upload scanned documents to a PaperLite cloud service. Scanned documents, page images, file names, favorites, and document metadata are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share a PDF using Android sharing options.

PaperLite is supported by ads provided through Google AdMob. Google and its partners may collect or receive device and advertising data to show ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and comply with applicable rules.

Information The App Stores Locally

PaperLite may store the following on your device:

  • Scanned document PDFs
  • Scanned page images
  • Document names
  • Page counts
  • Favorite status
  • Created and updated dates
  • OCR preview text returned by on-device scanning components, when available

This information is used to provide the app's document scanning, viewing, organizing, and sharing features.

Information Shared By The User

When you tap Share, Android lets you choose another app or destination. The selected destination may receive the PDF you choose to share. PaperLite does not control how another app handles the file after you share it.

Ads And Third-Party Services

PaperLite uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect data such as IP address, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location inferred by Google, app activity related to ad views or interactions, diagnostics, and other information described by Google's advertising and data policies.

Where required, PaperLite may show Google-provided consent or privacy options forms before requesting ads. These forms are provided by Google and are used to help manage advertising consent choices.

Learn more about how Google uses information from apps and sites that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Google AdMob privacy information: support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

No Account Or Cloud Sync

PaperLite does not provide user accounts, login, or PaperLite-operated cloud storage. The app does not intentionally upload scanned document contents to a PaperLite server.

Permissions

PaperLite uses document scanning functionality to capture pages with your device camera through Android and Google document scanning components. The app may also use Android share functionality when you choose to share a PDF.

Data Retention And Deletion

Scanned documents remain on your device until you delete them in the app or remove the app and its data from your device. Deleting a document in PaperLite removes the local PDF and related page images used by the app.

Ad-related data handled by Google is governed by Google's policies and controls.

Children

PaperLite is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children. Do not use the app to scan or share sensitive information about children unless you have the right to do so.
